“Let's Get Famous: The Video Game" takes the beloved board game created by Tony Rice and brings it into the digital realm, thanks to the remastering efforts of Daria Brown.

I'm currently developing a game based on a board game created by a friend. Using Unity, the game will feature a pixelated art style reminiscent of classic video games and will provide multiplayer functionality for players to enjoy and connect with one another.
Set within the competitive landscape of celebrity culture, players embark on a journey to stardom

while navigating through various scenes and challenges on the game board. There are fourteen diverse characters to choose from, each boasting a unique personality and aspirations that intensify the race to claim the spotlight and achieve A-list celebrity status.

The game currently includes a card function that randomizes cards for three players, each containing different challenges. For example, the "Strength Card" initiates a mini-game where players must perform random exercises. When one player fails, another player is prompted to push a button and receive a card. This continues until a winner is determined.

INTRODUCING SKILL 2/21/2024:

In Let's Get Famous players aren't just defined by their playing strategy; they also bring a diverse set of skills to the table. As individuals with unique careers and backgrounds, each player possesses a distinct combination of abilities that can greatly influence their success in the game. This is a nod to the real world that most people are more likely to succeed due to circumstances.

Luck: Ranges from 1 to 5, affecting the likelihood of getting better cards.

  • Luck 1: 50/50 chance
  • Luck 2: 45/55 chance
  • Luck 3: 40/60 chance
  • Luck 4: 35/65 chance
  • Luck 5: 30/70 chance

Wealth: Ranges from 1 to 5, representing the starting coins for each player.

  • Wealth 1: 100 Coins
  • Wealth 2: 200 Coins
  • Wealth 3: 300 Coins
  • Wealth 4: 400 Coins
  • Wealth 5: 500 Coins

Networking: Ranges from 1 to 5, affecting movement during activities.

  • Network 1: Moves 1x the card number
  • Network 2: Moves 1.25x the card number
  • Network 3: Moves 1.5x the card number
  • Network 4: Moves 1.75x the card number
  • Network 5: Moves 2.0x the card number

Talent: Ranges from 1 to 5, affecting the amount of coins earned.

  • Talent 1: Earns 1x the normal amount of coins
  • Talent 2: Earns 1.25x the normal amount of coins
  • Talent 3: Earns 1.50x the normal amount of coins
  • Talent 4: Earns 1.75x the normal amount of coins
  • Talent 5: Earns 2.0x the normal amount of coins

These attributes create a diverse set of player advantages and strategies within the game. Each individual will have up to 10 skill tacks. This tacks are not changeable in the game and allow each player to play depending of strengths. Examples: Daria would have more tacks in Luck, however, lack in wealth or talent. While Raeann could have more tacks in Wealth and Talent, but lack tacks within Networking or Luck. (subject to change)
