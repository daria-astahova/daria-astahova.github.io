Featured Project

Balloony Loony

An Unreal networked multiplayer game with vibes of Sky Pirates. This was a group project.

  • Role 3D Assets · VFX
  • ToolsUnreal · Blender
  • Duration Fall 2025
  • Team3 people
  • PlatformPC

Overview

Balloony Loony is a multiplayer game developed in Unreal Engine where players take on the roles of whimsical sky pirates navigating a vibrant world filled with floating islands and airships. Players can team up to complete quests, engage in aerial battles, and explore the skies in a cooperative multiplayer environment.

My Role & Contributions

  • Designing 3D Assets
  • Creating Visual Effects (VFX)

What I Learned

Working on Baloony Loony allowed me to deepen my skills in 3D asset creation and visual effects within Unreal Engine. Collaborating closely with a small team taught me lessons in communication and iterative design, ensuring that our assets not only looked great but also fit seamlessly into the game's aesthetic and technical requirements. I was never very strong with asset creation before this project, so I took this as an opportunity to push myself and learn new skills in Blender and Unreal Engine. Through trial and error, tutorials, and feedback from my teammates, I was able to create assets I am pround of.
