Vidma Les

An Unreal networked multiplayer game with vibes of Sky Pirates. This was a group project.

  • Role Developer
  • ToolsUnity · C# · Blender
  • Duration 2024 - Present
  • Team 1 person
  • Platform PC

Overview

Vidma Les is an atmospheric multiplayer exploration game set in a mystical forest where players assume the role of a newly appointed forest ranger. The game emphasizes cooperative exploration, environmental storytelling, and puzzle-solving as players work together to uncover the secrets of the enchanted woods while avoiding dangers that lurk within. Loosly based off of many slavic myths and folktales, you will encounter various mythical creatures and spirits as you take care of the forest. How you treat the forest is how it will treat you.

My Role & Contributions

  • Game Development using Unity and C#
  • Designing game mechanics and systems
  • Creating and integrating 3D assets using Blender
  • Collaborating on level design and environmental storytelling
  • Testing and debugging to ensure a smooth player experience

Process & Development

May 2025:

During the semester we were told to create assets for this game and use as many of our own created assets as possible. With limited experience in asset creation, I focused on creating simple yet effective models that fit the game's aesthetic. I ended up creating a detailed firepit for camping, a low poly tent, a bulletin board, Wooden bench, Statue, Wooden fence, and a low poly deer. I do hope to create more detailed and aesthetic models in the future.

What I Learned

I learned a lot about game development and asset creation throughout the process of working on Vidma Les. I improved my skills in Unity and C#, gaining a deeper understanding of game mechanics and systems. Additionally, I enhanced my 3D modeling skills using Blender, learning how to create assets that fit within a specific aesthetic and contribute to the overall atmosphere of the game. This project also taught me the importance of environmental storytelling and how to design levels that encourage exploration and engagement. Overall, working on Vidma Les has been a valuable experience that has helped me grow as a game developer.
