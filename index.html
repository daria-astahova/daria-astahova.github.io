About

I am a student at Boise State University finishing my Bachelor of Science in Games, Interactive Media, and Mobile Technology, with minors in Dance and Visual Arts.

I am passionate about sustainability, accessibility, movement-based learning, and community-driven work. I enjoy creating interactive experiences that encourage connection, creativity, and meaningful engagement through both technology and movement. I am excited to contribute my skills and enthusiasm to projects that make a positive impact. My goal is to create games and interactive media that are not only fun and engaging but also promote positive social and environmental change. I also really enjoy delving into storytelling and trying to create works of art that resonate with people on a deeper level but also are just plain enjoyable. I enjoy dipping my toes into various aspects of game development, from coding to 3D modeling to project management, and I am always eager to learn more and take on new challenges.
