About Daria Brown

Daria Brown is a student at Boise State University pursuing a Bachelor of Science degree in Games, Interactive Media, and Mobile Technology, and minors in both Visual Arts and Dance. Daria's primary passions are game design and animation, and she aspires to merge both disciplines into a fulfilling career. Motivated by a strong work ethic, creativity, and adept problem-solving skills, Daria is committed to continuous learning and growth. This website serves as a showcase of Daria's achievements and endeavors throughout her three-year journey at Boise State University.
