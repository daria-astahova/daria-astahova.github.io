Description:

Baller to the Waller was a paper and pencil game I made with three other GIMM students as part of an exercise in my Game Design Theory class. While I made the game concept with my classmates, I was the one who took the initiative to make the game digital. I used Visual Studios to create the game and used HTML, JavaScript, and CSS to make the game interactive. The game is a party game where up to five players can play and extended version of Tic Tac Toe. With this game you have to connect two walls together with your pieces. You have to have at least 3 pieces that are consistant and connect from one side of the board to another. Currently there are three shapes, a heart, a diamond, and a random shape. The game is still in development and I am working on adding more features to the game.
